Fan of Open Data?

You can download the entire website data. This is limited to once every week, per account.

Data will be in a single machine-readable JSON file. Pictures will be packed into a .zip file.

The data will include: Project title, short and long description, post date, votes, and comments.

Delete Your Data

Would you like to delete all your data from the website?

You can do this yourself, without filling out any form. All data associated with you (for example, name, address, phone, and e-mail data) will be deleted from the server, as well as the submissions and comments you have made.
